Faculty Information

Dr. Vijaya Kumar B P

VIJAY KUMAR B. P. Received the Ph. D degree in Electrical Communication Engg.,Department from Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore in 2003, M.Tech degree in Computer Science and Technology from the University of Roorkee (Presently Indian Institute of Technology, IITR), with honors in 1992 and Bacholers degree in Electronics and Communications from Mysore Univeristy with Distinction in the year 1987. He is currently a Professor and Head, in Information Science and Engg., Dept., MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore, Karnataka, India, where he is involved in research and teaching UG and PG students, and his major area of research are Computational Intelligence applications in Mobile, Adhoc and Sensor networks.
